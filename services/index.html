What We Do

Our Services

Professional, reliable gardening across Alderney. Every service delivered with 25+ years of experience behind it.

Lawn Care

Lawn Maintenance

A well-kept lawn transforms the feel of any garden. We provide regular mowing, edging and seasonal care to keep your grass in peak condition year-round.

Whether it's a small domestic plot or a large commercial space, we work to a consistent, high standard every visit.

Regular lawn mowing and edging
Lawn scarification and aeration
New lawn creation (seed or turf)
Seasonal lawn treatment

Trees

Tree Surgery

Our tree surgery service covers everything from routine pruning to full tree felling. We specialise in fruit tree care as well as larger ornamental and native species.

All work is carried out safely, efficiently and with full respect for your property and surroundings.

Fruit tree pruning and shaping
Crown reduction and thinning
Tree felling and stump removal
Tree planting and aftercare

Garden Design

Landscaping

From concept to completion, we transform outdoor spaces. Whether you want a completely new garden or improvements to what you already have, we bring expertise and vision to every project.

Garden design and planting plans
New lawn creation and flower beds
Weeding and garden clearance
Seasonal planting schemes

Hard Landscaping

Fencing & Patios

Quality hard landscaping to define and enhance your outdoor space. We install fencing and construct patios built to last, using materials suited to the island's climate.

Timber and panel fencing
Post and rail fencing
Patio design and construction
Garden boundary repairs

Hedges

Hedge Cutting

Neat, well-maintained hedges make a huge difference to the appearance of any property. We cut and shape all hedge types, from small garden dividers to large boundary hedging.

Box, privet and laurel hedges
Conifer and leylandii trimming
Formal and informal shaping
Hedge planting and establishment
