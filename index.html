About Us

Alderney's garden specialists since 1996.

Longis Bay Garden Services was founded by Jack Bingham in 1996. Jack still runs the business himself and has spent nearly 30 years getting to know the island's gardens, the local conditions and what grows well here.

Whether it is a lawn cut, a tree that needs attention or a full garden project, you will generally deal with Jack directly.
